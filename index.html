FAQ

Questions, answered.

Why switch from Buffer or Hootsuite?

Two reasons: price and people. Sync Socials costs a fraction of what the big tools charge, the interface is built for a 30-second posting workflow, and when you need help you talk to the founder directly — not a corporate ticket queue.

What platforms are supported?

Instagram, Facebook, YouTube and X / Twitter are live today. TikTok is coming soon, with more networks in progress.

How many posts can I schedule each month?

Unlimited. Schedule as many posts as you like across up to 10 accounts per platform — no per-post fees, ever.

Will my posts get less reach by scheduling them?

No. Posts are published natively through each platform's official API, exactly as if you posted them yourself — same reach, same engagement.

Can I cancel anytime?

Anytime, in one click, no questions asked. No contracts and no cancellation hoops.

Do I have to share my social passwords?

Never. You connect securely through each platform's official login (OAuth). Sync Socials never sees or stores your passwords.
