The rules

Simple, fair, no surprises.

Built to reward real creators and keep the program clean for everyone.

What counts as a paid referral?

A referral only counts once they become a paying Sync Socials customer. Free trials, canceled trials, refunded payments, failed payments, and chargebacks don't count.

The 90-day active rule

Milestone prizes unlock after referred customers stay active for 90 days. This protects the program from fake signups, churn, and gaming — so honest creators always get paid.

Annual vs. monthly customers

Annual subscribers count toward milestones once the refund window closes. Monthly subscribers count after they've stayed active for 90 days.

Prizes are cumulative

Hit 200 paid referrals and you earn $250 + $750 + $1,500 + $3,000 + $7,500 = $13,000 in total milestone prizes — plus your 30% recurring commission on top.

Cash or the item — your choice

For every tier, you choose: take the physical item or take its cash value. Some partners want the Rolex; others want the $7,500. Both are on the table.

Active count at verification

To unlock a tier you need that many active paid referrals at the time of verification. If you reach 100 but 8 cancel before their 90-day window, you're at 92 qualified referrals until you top back up.

Payout schedule

Commission is paid monthly. Milestone prizes are paid after the 90-day verification period. Each milestone bonus is paid once per partner; the 30% commission keeps recurring as long as your referrals keep paying.

No self-referrals or fraud

No self-referrals, fake accounts, coupon abuse, chargeback abuse, spam, misleading claims, or bidding on the Sync Socials brand name in paid ads without approval.
